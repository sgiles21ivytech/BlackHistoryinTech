Introduction

Throughout history, Black innovators have played a crucial role in shaping the technological landscape, yet their contributions are often overlooked in mainstream narratives. From groundbreaking advancements in computer science to transformative inventions in software and hardware, Black pioneers have continuously driven progress in the field of technology.

This website is dedicated to uncovering the hidden history of Black technologists in the United States, celebrating their achievements, and recognizing their invaluable impact on the digital world we rely on today.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to educate, inspire, and honor the legacy of Black innovators in technology. By shedding light on their contributions, we aim to provide a deeper understanding of their impact on modern computing and software development.
